<p><strong>Education and Career Guidance</strong>
</p>
<p>Education and Career Guidance (ECG) supports students in developing a
strong sense of purpose and direction in life. By nurturing self-awareness
and self-directedness, ECG helps students cultivate a growth mindset, adaptability,
and resilience, enabling them to embrace future opportunities and stay
relevant in a changing world.</p>
