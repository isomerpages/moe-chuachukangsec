<h2>Applied Learning Programme: Sustainable Living for a Resilient Future</h2>
<p><strong>Purpose:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To help CCKSians appreciate the relevance and value of what they are learning
in the academic curriculum to the real world.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To help CCKSians to develop stronger motivation and purpose to acquire
knowledge and skills.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Programme Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Excite Students in STEM</strong> through real-world contexts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Develop Critical, Adaptive and Inventive Thinkers</strong> through
student-centred pedagogies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Cultivate Design Thinkers</strong> through problem-solving and
problem finding</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Principle:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The emphasis is on the application of thinking skills, integrated knowledge
across subject disciplines, stretching the imagination and applying these
in the real-world settings in society and industries.</p>
</li>
</ul>
