<p>Badminton is an established CCA in Chua Chu Kang Secondary School and
aims to equip students with the skills and techniques required to play
badminton at a competitive level, including proper footwork, racket skills
and strategic gameplay.</p>
<p>Students will get the opportunity to participate in matches and competitions
allowing them to apply the skills they have learnt in a competitive environment.</p>
<p>Besides the physical benefits of playing badminton, the CCA emphasises
on and provides students with opportunities to develop values and life
skills such as teamwork, discipline and time management.</p>
<p>Overall, Badminton CCA is a great way for students to develop their badminton
skills while also gaining valuable life skills and experiences.</p>
