<h3><strong>School Traffic Management Plan</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>Introduction</strong></h4>
<p>Chua Chu Kang Secondary School is located at 31 Teck Whye Crescent, Singapore
688848. Due to the proximity of Teck Whye Primary School (TWPS) and Jurong
Pioneer Junior College (JPJC), morning traffic can be heavy.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/About Us/School Traffic Management Plan/Image01.jpg">
</div>
<h4><strong>Aim</strong></h4>
<p>Our traffic management plan ensures the safety of students, staff, and
visitors while minimizing congestion and promoting orderly traffic flow
within school premises.</p>
<h4><strong>Key Considerations</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Ensure safe usage of school roads</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Facilitate safe entry and exit for pedestrians and vehicles</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minimize traffic congestion and ensure smooth flow</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>School’s Operating Hours</strong></h4>
<p><u>For Pedestrians and Cyclists</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Main Entrance: 6:30 AM - 6:00 PM (Mon-Fri)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Side Gate 7 (Pang Sua Park Connector): 7:00 AM - 8:00 AM (Mon-Fri)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><u>For Vehicles</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Main Entrance (except Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Peak Hours: 6:30 AM - 8:00 AM (Mon-Fri) - OPEN (Barrier UP)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Off-Peak Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Mon-Fri) - OPEN (Barrier DOWN)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>Transportation to School</strong></h4>
<p><u>Public Transport</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Buses: 190, 975, 985 (4-6 mins walk from nearest bus stop)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bus 983M: School term weekdays only, arrives at the school at <strong>6:59 AM</strong> and <strong>7:19 AM</strong> respectively</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>MRT:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Choa Chu Kang Station (NS4) - 21 mins walk</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bukit Panjang Station (DT1) – 18 mins walk</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>LRT: Phoenix Station (BP5) - 11 mins walk</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><u>By Car</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enter Teck Whye Crescent via Masjid Al-Khair Mosque and Choa Chu Kang
Polyclinic to avoid congestion.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/About Us/School Traffic Management Plan/Image02.jpg">
</div>
<p><u>Cycling</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Register bicycles at the general office with parental consent</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use proper safety equipment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Dismount before entering school compound</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adhere to safety rules to avoid cycling ban</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>Safety Measures</strong></h4>
<p><u>For Pedestrians/Cyclists</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Use traffic lights at Teck Whye Walk and Teck Whye Crescent junction</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Follow road marshals' signals at the main entrance</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use zebra crossings within school compound</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cyclists must dismount and push bicycles when entering</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/About Us/School Traffic Management Plan/Image03.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<p><u>For Vehicles (6:30 AM - 8:00 AM)</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enter via Teck Whye Crescent (Masjid Al-Khair Mosque/CCK Polyclinic route)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Observe 15km/hr speed limit within school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use designated drop-off zone at school porch</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exit via roundabout at main entrance</p>
</li>
</ul>
