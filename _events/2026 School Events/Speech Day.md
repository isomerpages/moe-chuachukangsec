<h1><strong>Growing Together, Thriving Together: CCKS Speech Day 2026</strong></h1>
<p>Chua Chu Kang Secondary School recently celebrated its 2026 Speech Day,
a vibrant afternoon dedicated to honoring the collective achievements of
our CCKSians, staff, and partners. Guided by the theme "Growing Together,
Thriving Together," the event was a powerful reflection of our school vision:
Nurturing CCKSians with Future-ready Mindsets and Empathetic Hearts who
Act Responsibly.</p>
<h4><strong><em>Principal’s Address: A Culture of Excellence</em></strong></h4>
<p>In his opening address, our Principal, Mr. Timothy Cheng, reflected deeply
on how the school has cultivated a vibrant ecosystem where success is a
collective effort. He highlighted that "thriving" is a result of the spirit
of continuous learning among our teachers and the collaborative energy
of students who engage in interactive learning every day.</p>
<p>Mr. Cheng paid tribute to our Parent Support Group, whose active involvement
in milestones like Racial Harmony Day, National Day, Graduation Day, and
the Sports Challenge strengthens our school spirit. Beyond our gates, he
noted how CCKSians act with Care and Respect by giving back through SG
Cares and activities conducted at Bethany Home by our Student Leaders.
From empowering minds through global exchange trips to China, Japan, Thailand,
and Indonesia, to building a resilient future through our Applied Learning
Programme (ALP) on sustainable living, Mr. Cheng emphasized that every
experience—whether in outdoor education or community service—is designed
to build a deep sense of pride, belonging, and an enterprising spirit.</p>
<h4><strong><em>A Symphony of Excellence</em></strong></h4>
<p>The ceremony opened with a stirring performance by the School Concert
Band, setting the stage with the adventurous melodies of <em>Pirates of the Caribbean</em>.
This spirit of boldness and harmony carried through the afternoon as we
recognized our top academic achievers from the 2025 GCE ‘O’, ‘N(A)’, and
‘N(T)’ Level examinations.</p>
<p>A particularly moving moment was the presentation of the Resilient Achievers
Award, honoring students who demonstrated incredible fortitude in overcoming
personal challenges. Their stories reminded us that Resilience remains
the bedrock of growth at CCKS.</p>
<h4><strong><em>Leadership and Inspiration: Student of the Year</em></strong></h4>
<p>The highlight of the individual awards was the Student of the Year 2025,
awarded to Chin Ping Yi. In a heartfelt citation, Ping Yi was lauded for
embodying the school's mission to thrive and contribute. In his acceptance
speech, he shared a powerful message on Agency:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>"Your dreams may shift along the way and that’s okay. It’s the journey, the experience, that reveals what truly matters to you."</em>
</p>
</blockquote>
<h4><strong><em>Celebrating Our Pillars of Support</em></strong></h4>
<p>Speech Day also honors the community that makes student success possible.
Mr. Cheng presented several key awards:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Staff Promotions:</strong> We celebrated our teachers and executive
and administrative staff who were promoted this year, marking their professional
growth and expanded contributions to the school’s mission.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Long Service Awards:</strong> We expressed our deepest gratitude
to our long-serving staff for their unwavering dedication and the years
they have spent nurturing generations of CCKSians.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Friend of the School Awards:</strong> We honored our valued community
partners and the Parent Support Group, whose collaborative spirit provides
the essential support system our students need to excel.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>To mark the occasion, a special foil embossing artwork titled "Flourishing
Together"—created by student artists Lim Xin Hui Caydia (3A) and Eva Megan
Dsouza (3B) — was presented to our Guest of Honour, Dr. Tay Choon Nghee.</p>
<h4><strong><em>Thriving Through the Arts and Action</em></strong></h4>
<p>The theme of unity was brought to life through breathtaking performances:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Momentum (Modern Dance): </strong>Their piece, inspired by the
resilient <em>Rafflesia</em> and <em>Ivy</em>, used fluid formations to show
the beauty of standing strong together.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Wushu &amp; Taekwondo: </strong>A combined showcase that bridged
the grace of traditional martial arts with modern precision, symbolizing
the strength that comes from combined efforts.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong><em>Closing with a Shared Vision</em></strong></h4>
<p>As the school sang the School Song in unison, the sense of pride was palpable.
By celebrating our milestones, we reaffirm our commitment to being Ethical
and Adaptable CCKSians who seek opportunities for growth and act with empathy.
As we move forward, we remain dedicated to the idea that we are at our
best when we are Growing Together and Thriving Together.</p>
