<h2><strong>A Vibrant Tapestry of Tradition: Celebrating Hari Raya at Our School</strong></h2>
<p>This year’s Hari Raya Puasa celebration was a spectacular showcase of
community and culture, centered around an exciting assembly program that
brought the entire school to its feet.</p>
<h4><strong><em>A Festive Welcome: Teachers Leading the Way</em></strong></h4>
<p>Walking into the school, the festive spirit was immediately palpable.
The school foyer had been decorated with the theme - <em>Kampung</em> wonderland.
This stunning display—featuring intricate <em>ketupat</em> hangings, vibrant <em>batik </em>fabrics,
and informative boards—was the result of the hard work by our dedicated
teachers. Their efforts created the perfect backdrop for photos and set
a warm, nostalgic tone for the day's events, proving that our educators
are as creative as they are instructional.</p>
<h4><strong><em>The Power of Parent Support</em></strong></h4>
<p>While the teachers handled the aesthetics, our parent community provided
the backbone of support for the morning’s program. Cheering loudly from
the audience, their presence was a vital reminder of the strong partnership
between home and school. Their encouragement turned a standard school assembly
into a true family affair, filled with smiles and a shared sense of pride.</p>
<h4><strong><em>The Heartbeat of the Assembly: Dikir Barat</em></strong></h4>
<p>The undisputed highlight of the assembly was the spectacular <em>Dikir Barat</em> performance.
Our CCKSian troupe took the stage, dressed in stunning, <em>Baju Melayu</em> and <em>samping</em>.</p>
<p>The energy was electric. The <em>Tukang Karang</em> (lead singer) commanded
the hall with verses of joy and unity, while the <em>Awak-Awak</em> (chorus)
responded with thunderous, rhythmic clapping and perfectly synchronized
hand movements. The precision of their performance was a testament to weeks
of grueling rehearsals. As the beat accelerated, the entire school joined
in, clapping along in a moment of pure, collective harmony. It was a powerful
display of Malay cultural heritage that left everyone inspired.</p>
<h4><strong><em>More Than Just a Celebration</em></strong></h4>
<p>The assembly programme also featured cultural performances where performers
in their finest <em>Baju Kurung</em> and <em>Baju Melayu</em>, turned the
hall into a sea of shimmering silks and intricate patterns.</p>
<p>Beyond the performances and the beautiful decorations, the day carried
a deeper meaning. It was a celebration of forgiveness, unity, and the strengthening
of bonds. By coming together to witness the talents of our CCKSians and
the hard work of our teachers and parents, we did more than just mark a
festival—we strengthened the heartbeat of our school community.</p>
<p>The echoes of the <em>Dikir Barat</em> may have faded, but the festive cheer
and the spirit of togetherness will certainly carry us through the rest
of the school year.</p>
<p><strong>Selamat Hari Raya Maaf Zahir dan Batin!</strong>
