<h2><strong>Chua Chu Kang Secondary School Open House 2025: Celebrating Community, Curiosity and Connections</strong></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Proposed_CCKSS_Open_House_2024__2000_x_875_052_mm___1000_x_1750_104_mm_.jpg">
</div>
<p>Chua Chu Kang Secondary School (CCKSS) welcomed prospective students and
families to its annual Open House, bringing the campus to life with vibrant
showcases, engaging activities, and warm conversations. This year’s event
was especially meaningful as it reflected our 2025 school theme, <strong>“Together We Excel”</strong>,
highlighting the power of unity, collaboration, and shared achievement
within the CCKSS community.</p>
<h3><strong>A Warm Welcome to Our Visitors</strong></h3>
<p>Guests were greeted by our friendly and dedicated student ambassadors
who guided them through the school’s facilities and learning spaces. Along
the way, visitors had the opportunity to speak with teachers and learn
more about CCKSS’s holistic approach to education—balancing academic excellence
with strong values, character development, and student well-being.</p>
<p>Through these interactions, guests experienced firsthand how the CCKSS
community embodies our theme, working together to support every student
in reaching their full potential.</p>
<h3><strong>Vibrant Showcases by CCAs and Signature Programmes</strong></h3>
<p>A major highlight of the Open House was the lively array of booths and
demonstrations by our Co-Curricular Activities (CCAs) and signature programmes.
Students proudly shared their experiences, achievements, and learning journeys
through hands-on activities and interactive displays.</p>
<p>Our <strong>Signature Programmes</strong> were featured prominently, including:</p>

<p>These showcases truly captured the spirit of <em>“Together We Excel”</em> as
students and staff worked side by side to present the rich opportunities
available at CCKSS.</p>
<h3><strong>Parent Support Group: Strengthening Home–School Partnership</strong></h3>
<p>The <strong>Parent Support Group (PSG)</strong> also played a key role in
the Open House, hosting a welcoming booth where parent volunteers shared
their experiences supporting school initiatives and building a strong parent
community. Their presence highlighted the importance of home–school collaboration—an
essential part of our belief that when we work together, every student
excels.</p>
<h3><strong>A Collective Effort, A Memorable Day</strong></h3>
<p>The success of the event was a testament to the dedication and teamwork
of the entire CCKSS family—students, staff, parents, and alumni. Their
unity and enthusiasm created an inspiring atmosphere, perfectly reflecting
our 2025 school theme.</p>
<h3><strong>Looking Forward</strong></h3>
<p>CCKSS extends its heartfelt appreciation to all visitors who joined us
at the Open House. We hope the experience offered a meaningful glimpse
into the vibrant, supportive, and forward-looking environment that awaits
new students.</p>
<p>As we move ahead into 2025, we look forward to welcoming more members
into our CCKSS community!</p>
