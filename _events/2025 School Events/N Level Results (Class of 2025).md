<h2><strong>Celebrating the Release of the 2025 N-Level Examination Results</strong></h2>
<p>The 2025 Singapore-Cambridge GCE Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical)
Level Examination Results were released on 18 December 2025, marking an
important milestone for our CCKSians and the school community.</p>
<p>This achievement reflects our CCKSians’ strong embodiment of the school
values of Respect, Resilience, Integrity, Care and Enterprise. Throughout
the academic year, they demonstrated resilience in overcoming challenges,
integrity in their learning, and respect for their teachers, peers and
the learning environment. Many showed care by supporting one another, while
exercising enterprise by taking ownership of their goals and striving for
continuous improvement.</p>
<p>We are proud of our CCKSians for their perseverance, positive attitudes
and commitment to personal growth. Their N-Level journey has not only strengthened
their academic foundation, but also shaped them into confident learners
with strong character and values.</p>
<p>The school extends its sincere appreciation to our dedicated teachers
for their guidance and mentorship, and to parents and guardians for their
steadfast support and encouragement.</p>
<p>As we celebrate this meaningful milestone, we reaffirm our belief in every
CCKSian’s potential. Guided by our values and equipped with confidence
and determination, our CCKSians are well-prepared to take their next steps
and pursue their aspirations.</p>
<p><strong>Congratulations to the Class of 2025! We are proud of your achievements and wish you every success in the journey ahead.</strong>
</p>
