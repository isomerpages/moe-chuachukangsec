During Discovery Week, Secondary One students engaged in programming and electronics, acquiring skills essential for the ever-changing world of technology. They also participated in beach cleanups, promoting environmental awareness through various projects. Secondary Two and Three students visited Childcare Centres and the Lifelong Learning Institute, gaining practical exposure and a broader understanding of educational and career pathways. Ice-cream sharing sessions fostered unity and belonging within each class. Parent-teacher conferences encouraged reflection, communication, and collaboration for the benefit of students' academic success and overall well-being.
