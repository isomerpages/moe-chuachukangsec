<h4>Financial Assistance Scheme for Singapore Citizen Students</h4>
<p>Singapore Citizen students from households facing financial difficulties
may receive assistance from the Ministry of Education (MOE) to help cover
school fees and other education related expenses.</p>
<p>For more details on the MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS), please
visit:</p>
<p>👉 <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance</a>
</p>
<p><strong>How to Apply for MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS) 2026</strong>
</p>
<p>Parents may submit the application digitally through either of the following
channels:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Parents Gateway app &gt; Services &gt; Financial Assistance), or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Online application portal</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>👉 <a href="https://go.gov.sg/moe-efasggas" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/moe-efasggas</a>
</p>
<p></p>
<h4>MOE Group Personal Accident (GPA) Insurance Plan for Students</h4>
<p>All students in government and government aided schools are automatically
covered under the MOE Group Personal Accident (GPA) Insurance Plan at no
cost to parents.</p>
<p>This insurance provides coverage for accidents that may occur:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>During school hours</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>During school related activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>While travelling directly to and from school</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Coverage is subject to the terms, conditions, and claim limits of the
policy.</p>
<p>Details on coverage, claims procedures, frequently asked questions (FAQs),
and other related information are available on Income’s <a href="https://www.income.com.sg/group-insurance-for-schools-and-moe-personnel/group-personal-accident-for-students" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">website</a>.</p>
<p>Parents are advised to submit claims <a href="https://studentgpa.incomegroupins.com.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">online</a>.</p>
